David Herdeiro en/pt Back to the beginning 2024 Jan. 13 Four Years of In-House Design During the past four and a half years I had the pleasure of working within the Brand and Communication Team from Turbine Kreuzberg. During this time I had the opportunity to explore several areas, focusing primarly on design but also on Social Media Management, Copy, Brand Awareness and Employer Branding.

Here’s a short, image based, presentation of some of this work. * * *

Design

Social Media Design at the company always had a very specific look & feel, majorly focused on the brand’s typography. Also, being a B2B Agency, we were tasked with finding a balance between possible clients and curious applicants for job vacancies, while retaining some type of interest from the Developing community.


Explainers

Short and visually simple explanations of tech concepts, this could go from AI Features to Agile Methodologies.

2023 Recap

A nice little project we used to jump on the Spotify’s Recap train.

Case Studies

Primarily focused on getting lead’s attention, we were tasked with showing what we did with other clients and what type of services we can provide, with real case examples.

Website Redesign

During some of my free-time, I decided to explore new ways that we could present ourselves on our own platform, focusing of getting traffic to our Services pages.

Video

One of our preferred type of content were videos. During short videos, typically up to six minutes, and almost never more than ten, we would present tech concepts, present the team members and positions, talk about new services, …


Testimonials

Github Copilot Video Series

Brand Awareness

A considerable portion of my work was to take care of the design for external events that we hosted through the country (and sometimes, out!)
This assets were specially funny to me because I had the freedom of creating both the design and the copywriting used on it, which allowed me to explore a bit more of my creative writing style.
