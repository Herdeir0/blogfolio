Think your Notion databases ahead Only if you want to do it right.

Imagine this: You’re given a circle on a square canvas. What can you do with it if you can only use black and white? Surely not much. A black circle on a white background, or a white circle on a black background.
By itself, it doesn’t mean much, doesn’t it? Perhaps the white circle is the moon in the night sky. Maybe the black circle on the white background is… Well, a dot on white paper.

Then something clicked on me: You can’t break Notion (unless you really try)
It was the perfect tool to lose myself and experiment different types of organization. There’s tables, there’s databases, you can embed other platforms within, you can upload lossless images and videos, searching filters, rules and automations and much, much more. All within the same infinite page, just a click away. But all of these were just experiments, there wasn’t really no reason behind them… Until I realized that my team was fighting something that I could solve.
