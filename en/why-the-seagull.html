Why the Seagull - David Herdeiro Why the Seagull? Why the Seagull? Like a seagull that flights south during the winter, I too feel like I'm migrating from an experience to another.

2024 was the year for new experiences.

I started Diálogos de Design with some friends, and we had the absolute blast during these three events so far; Made the Adobe switch and become an official Affinity and DaVinci Resolve fan; Had the chance to try new design fields, which led me to discover that I do enjoy web and app design; I digged further into creating my own lens for my homemade digital camera; Was part of the design team for a TEDx event, as well as their livestream guy; Focused more time on learning about Generative AI and Design Ethics; And I started developing this website, which proved to be a nice experience that thaught me how to deploy a simple website, and pushed me on improving my frontend skills.

And now, in 2025, I start a new journey, a new migration.
